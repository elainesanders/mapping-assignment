Are Teen Girls Getting Married?

Numbers of teen marriages have been going down

The number of teen girls in marriages or unions has been going down since 1970. This data set was collected by the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs. In 1970, the highest percentage of married young women was in Niger, with 77.6% of married women of reproductive age being 15-19 years old.
